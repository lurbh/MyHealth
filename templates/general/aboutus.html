About Us

Who we Are

An up and coming start-up, MyHealth is forged from the bonds made during our Final year project, upon which we’ve decided to further develop our project into something much more, something viable, something solid, concrete for the healthcare industry to rely on. We have evolved from a simple project into a full-blown business with exponential growth and propects.

At MyHealth we are dedicated into serving the needs of our customers, as a catalyst for the growth of the healthcare industry and to promote the health and well-being of the populace for the good of the world.

As part of our growth plan, we are currently aiming to become an icon of the booking for health facilities in Singapore, becoming a household name in Singapore, eventually the whole of Asia, and maybe the world.

MyHealth is now solidly positioned for growth and expansion, a pilot program is on the way that serves prove our effectiveness and reliability. Thus, will pave the way for further expansion and contracts.

This is just the beginning.

Why Choose Us?

MyHealth is extensively tested with numerous stress-based performance assessments to ensure top of the line performance and convenience. Giving you the piece of mind that MyHealth is for sure to be reliable and will be able to function rain or shine.

Our team has the expertise and resources in order to provide support and continuous development for our services. With close support available 24/7 and an intelligent approach to the business what’s there not to like?

Always improving, always evolving choose Myhealth!
